    DETAILED INFORMATION

    Sons of Korah International (Benekora) is a youth congregation with a purpose of spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and it gathers all the youth together to motivate them to know about God, changing their mindsets, bringing back the value of the Rwandan culture as well as to be exemplary to others in moral values and behaviors. The visionaries are Muzigura Jackson and Gabriel Dubier and they started working in 2014. After recognizing that the youth have an issue of misconduct whether in their families and in the society, and that there are some Christians that are not strong in their salvation, therefore the passion arose of solving these problems in the youth by doing a follow up for the in their schools and outside of Kigali. So far they have a record of 2,075 saved boys and girls in Sons of Korah in all the provinces of the country from different churches. More 15,154 people were preached the gospel in their concerts in 3 years (2014-2017). And now they started different projects that will provide an income for financial development as well as businesses in Agriculture and through this they provide jobs among themselves so as to decrease the rate of unemployment. Now they have kids who have testimonies of their transformation and thus became boys and girls who changed their mindsets. They have now started working and cooperating with the government in different activities of teaching the youth and changing their mindsets. Currently they have an office in Kicukiro district in the cell of Kigarama.
